## The Plan (In Plain English)

### Step 1: Make Google Love Your Website (Week 1-2)

**What we're doing:** Adding behind-the-scenes code that tells Google exactly what your business does, where you operate, and what services you offer.

**Why it matters:** Google uses this information to decide who shows up in search results. Without it, Google is guessing — and it guesses wrong.

**What you'll see:** Nothing visible changes on the website, but Google will start understanding your business much better.

---

### Step 2: Create Location Pages (Week 2-4)

**What we're doing:** Building dedicated pages for each area you service — Newcastle, Lake Macquarie, Maitland, Port Stephens, Hunter Valley, and Cessnock. Then key suburbs like Merewether, Nelson Bay, Charlestown, etc.

**Why it matters:** When someone searches "smart home installer Maitland" — if you don't have a page about Maitland, you won't show up. Your competitors don't have these pages either, so this puts you ahead.

**What you'll see:** New pages on the website like:
- onpointautomation.com.au/areas/newcastle
- onpointautomation.com.au/areas/port-stephens
- onpointautomation.com.au/areas/hunter-valley
- ...and more

Each page talks about smart home solutions specific to that area — not just copy-paste with the town name swapped (Google penalises that).

---

### Step 3: Get Found by AI Assistants (Month 1-2)

**What we're doing:** Structuring your website content so that when people ask ChatGPT, Google AI, or Siri "who installs Control4 in Newcastle?" — Onpoint Automation comes up as the answer.

**Why it matters:** AI-referred searches grew **527% last year**. This is the future of how people find businesses. Right now, almost nobody in your industry is optimised for this. First mover advantage is massive.

**What you'll see:**
- FAQ pages answering common questions ("How much does a smart home cost?", "Control4 vs Crestron")
- Comparison guides and how-to content
- Your business being recommended when people ask AI assistants

---

### Step 4: Get Listed Everywhere (Month 1-3)

**What we're doing:** Making sure Onpoint Automation is listed consistently across all the places that matter:

- **Google Business Profile** (the box that shows up when people Google you — with reviews, photos, phone number)
- **Apple Maps & Bing**
- **Australian directories** — Yellow Pages, True Local, HiPages, Oneflare, ServiceSeeking
- **Industry directories** — Control4 Dealer Locator, CEDIA
- **Review sites** — ProductReview.com.au, Houzz

**Why it matters:** The more places your business appears with consistent information, the more Google and AI assistants trust you. Businesses listed on 10+ platforms are **3x more likely** to be recommended by AI.

**What you need to do:**
- After each job, ask the customer to leave a Google review (we can set up an easy link/QR code)
- Aim for 1-2 new reviews per week
- Target: 4.8+ star rating

---

### Step 5: Start a Blog & YouTube (Ongoing)

**What we're doing:** Creating helpful content that positions you as the expert:

- Blog posts: "Complete Guide to Smart Home Automation in Newcastle", "Smart Lighting: 5 Ways It Saves You Money"
- YouTube videos: installation walkthroughs, smart home demos, product comparisons

**Why it matters:**
- Google ranks websites higher when they regularly publish helpful content
- YouTube is the **#1 most-cited source** by AI assistants
- It builds trust with potential customers before they even call you

**What you need to do:** Share photos and videos from jobs (even phone quality is fine). We'll turn them into polished content.

---

### Step 6: Showcase Your Credentials (Ongoing)

**What we're doing:** Making sure your expertise is front and centre:

- Control4 Platinum Dealer status prominently displayed
- Project galleries with before/after photos
- Case studies showing real installations
- Team bios with certifications

**Why it matters:** Google specifically looks for proof that you're experienced, expert, and trustworthy. AI assistants weight this even more heavily — **96% of AI recommendations** come from sources with verified credentials.
