# Onpoint Automation — SEO Plan (Summary)

## The Problem
When someone Googles "smart home installer Newcastle" or asks ChatGPT "who installs Control4 near me?" — you don't show up. Your competitors do.

## The Fix

### 1. Get Found Locally
We're building dedicated pages for each area you service — Newcastle, Lake Macquarie, Maitland, Port Stephens, Hunter Valley, Cessnock. Your competitors don't have these. This alone is a massive advantage.

### 2. Get Recommended by AI
ChatGPT, Google AI, and Siri are how more and more people find businesses. We're structuring your site so these tools recommend Onpoint Automation by name.

### 3. Get Listed Everywhere
Google Business Profile, Yellow Pages, HiPages, Control4 directory, and 10+ other platforms — all with consistent info. More listings = more trust = higher rankings.

### 4. Get Reviews Flowing
After each job, send customers a quick link to leave a Google review. Target: 4.8+ stars. This is the single biggest factor for showing up in local search.

- Photos from jobs (phone quality is fine)
- Ask happy customers for Google reviews
- Share project stories for case studies
